ABOUT THE PROJECT

This is our first project as students at Wild Code School Lisbon campus. Our theme is "Lx BEER GUIDE" for this project and we developed the content, putting in practice our skills learned in class about CSS, HTML.

Our main goal is to make beer lovers know the best brewers in the region. In addition, the idea is to make the local trade stronger by making our partners known worldwide.

Now you have a new beer mate in Lisbon!
